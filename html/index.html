who we are

At kindRoot Foundation, we believe that every soul deserves love, diginity, and care
- No matter their background, age, or situation.

We are a community-driven non-profit dedicated to reaching society's most vulnerable: The elderly, orphans, neglected children,widows, and individuals often forgotten. Our mission is to restore hope, meet needs, and build a network of compassion rooted in kindness.

what we do

  1. - Support the Elderly: We provide companionship, basic necessities, and medical assistance.
  2. - Empower Widows: Through skills training, counselling, and economic support.
  3. - Care for Orphans and Neglected children: By partnering with homes and communities to ensure education, safety, and emotional well-being.
  4. - Advocate for the disabled: Creating awareness and support systems, skills empowerment for the disabled and shaping a future for them.

Join us

Whether by volunteering, donating, or spreading the word, you can become a part of this mission. Together, we can make kindness a foundation that changes lives by building a world where kindness is the root of every action - lifing the weak, healing the broken, and strengthening communities.
