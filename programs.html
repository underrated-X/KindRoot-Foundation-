our core programs

Elderly Care

  • Challenges: Elderly ones are often denied needs and care, with little to no affection. This often leads to sucidial actions as a result of loneliness, and inadequate care
  • Solution: Provide shelter, care and love by providing thems with care packages, engaging them in healthy activities, visits and medical aids
  • How this can help: By providing adequate care, medical supports, healthy activities, this will reduce loneliness and help them live a healthy fulfilled live incorporated with beautiful memories

Orphans support

  • Challenges: Lack of basic needs such as healthy and nutrientous food, healthy lifestyle, healthcare and Education.
  • Solution: Provide foods and nutrients, clean clothes and healthy environment, top-notch education, and emotional care
  • How this can help: By doing this, it will help raise healthy, educated and ingenious children in the society and reduce child crimes

Neglected Children

  • Challenges: child abuse, emotional abuse, denied love and parental care
  • Solution: Safe place, therapy sessions, and emotional bonding
  • How this can help: This will help conquer their emotional damages, ignite love in them, expose them to a more vibrant and meaningful life

Widow Empowerment

  • Challenges: Emotional instability, economic instability, Society discrimination and gender-based limitations
  • Solution: Skills training, micro support, and emotional healing.
  • How this can help: This will kindled hope, make life worth living, reduce sucidial rates among widows and help the reshape their lives

Disabled Inclusion

  • Challenges: Limited accessibility to physical structures, Exclusion in employment/programs, social exclusion and public misconceptions
  • Solution:Medial tools i.e arm chairs, clutches etc, education, inclusive activities, vocational training, and job empowerment.
  • How this can help: This will help equipped the disables, provide a healthy life and make them feel valuable and loved
